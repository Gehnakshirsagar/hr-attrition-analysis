# hr-attrition-analysis
Exploring why employees leave companies using Power BI and HR data

## What I Did
I analyzed an IBM HR dataset of 1,470 employees to understand attrition patterns across departments, age groups, job roles, and income levels.

This was my first time using Power BI so I had to do some research along the way to figure out how to load data and build visuals. It took some trial and error but I got there.

## What the Dashboard Shows
- 16% of employees left the company while 84% stayed
- Managers earn the highest average monthly income, Sales Representatives the least
- Average employee age is similar across all departments at around 35-37 years old
- Most employees are between 25 and 45 years old with the peak around age 35

## Dataset
HR Employee Attrition dataset from Kaggle.
Download and place the CSV in the data folder to explore the data yourself.

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop for the dashboard
- Excel to explore the data before building visuals
